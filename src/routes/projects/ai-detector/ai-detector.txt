All vision models are powered by the open-source AI Detector software, developed by E. Schouten.

the AI Detector

AI Detector is an advanced, AI-powered detection system designed to monitor video streams in real time and alert you whenever something relevant is detected. It includes an intelligent double-check mechanism to help reduce false positives and improve reliability.

What it does

At Cowcatcher AI, we train and develop the vision models. The AI Detector is the software layer that applies these models in practice—using their learned knowledge to perform accurate, real-world detections.
The detector could also be used outside the Cowcatcher provides models and used for your own projects for example, alerting for people, cars, tractors pulling up etc. 

The system provides the following capabilities:

Continuously monitors one or multiple cameras, as well as video files.
Detects objects using a YOLO model, which is fast and runs locally without requiring cloud processing.
Optionally verifies detections by asking an AI a custom question (for example: “Is there really a person?”). This step helps filter out false alarms but can be skipped if not needed.
Sends alerts through Telegram, saves images or video recordings to disk, or triggers external systems via webhooks.
Components

Detector
The core service of the system. It handles object detection and optional AI-based verification. The entire system is configurable through a single config.json file, making it flexible and easy to manage.

Webb-app
A web-based interface currently under development. It will allow users to monitor live video streams, review past detections, and manage system activity through a user-friendly dashboard. The interface will also make it easy to configure settings and connect your cameras.
